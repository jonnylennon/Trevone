About us
who we are

Trevone Consulting was founded by Alison Lennon in 2012 to assist SMEs in the South West region with their people and employment challenges. Alison is a senior HR professional with extensive experience across a number of business & industry sectors including but not limited to Government & public services, private sector and Small to Medium Enterprise. Alison is a commercially focused professional who holds a BA (hons) Modern Languages, Post Graduate Diploma in Human Resources & is a Member of the Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development.
